\# LAWS OF AURORA



\## Purpose



Aurora is not a traditional game.



Aurora is a planetary emergence simulator.



Every system must exist because nature allows it to exist.



Nothing exists merely because a programmer decided so.



\---



\# LAW I



Nothing emerges before the environment allows it.



\---



\# LAW II



No system may violate the fundamental laws of the world.



\---



\# LAW III



Every natural phenomenon must leave consequences.



Nothing disappears without changing the world.



\---



\# LAW IV



Existence precedes classification.



Things exist first.



Names come later.



\---



\# LAW V



The order of emergence is determined by natural dependencies.



Never by programmer preference.



\---



\# LAW VI



Every natural system must be measurable before it influences another.



Observation precedes interaction.



\---



\## Development Philosophy



The engine simulates causes.



The game shows consequences.



\---



\## Core Principle



The player should never feel that the world was scripted.



The player should feel that the world became what it is.
